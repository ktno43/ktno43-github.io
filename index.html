About Me

Hey, my name is Kyle if you didn't catch that already as the page suggests. I am a recent graduate from California State University Northridge with a B.S in Computer Science. I come from a family of software engineers who have worked all over the industry and I am looking to continue the tradition by following in their footsteps and finding a career within this field. The years of experience that they have learned while working in the industry are passed down to me which makes me the capable software developer I am today.

In the field of work, software development is a puzzle to me that I can't get enough of. Developing is fun and extraordinarily satisfying when you are able to get portions of your project running and functional. I believe there is always a solution to a problem since computers are exactly like us, if you can think of the solution then you can implement it.

Having that kind of mentality allows me to problem solve better, adapt to, and redesign solutions to situations that I am unable to understand at first glance. Aside from coding, eating, drinking coffee, and sleeping I may have somewhat of a social life. I play video games, watch TV shows and sports, and I am really into the techy gadgets and gizmos.

Thanks for stopping by whoever you may be whether it be a friend, family member, or future employer!
